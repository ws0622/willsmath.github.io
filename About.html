About Me

I was born in Pensacola, Florida where my father was an airline mechanic and my mother was a school nurse. Around 2009 my dad got offered a position at Delta Airlines, this caused us to move to Newnan, Georgia where I spent most of my childhood. This is where I found my love for computers and pretty much anything IT related. I spent most of my free time in middle and High school trying to tinker with my computer or build other peoples PCs for spending money. Around High School is when I found my love for cars and basketball as well, so most of my free time consisted of playing basketball, working on computers, or helping my dad tinker with his car.

All of my friends ended up going to Kennesaw State because of their well known IT program, so I followed suit and went there as well. Now I am in my fourth year here and have loved every second of it! I have also been involved in a few extracurricular activities including the Rec Sports leagues that KSU offers along with working at AutoZone Part-Time for the past 3 years. Now I am actively looking for any sort of internship to get more experience and to learn more about the field.
